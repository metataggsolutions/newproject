Insurance Programs

Effective Outreach, Secure Solutions

Coverdell has been marketing insurance products for over 30 years and was one of the first companies to market insurance through financial institutions. Our insurance products are underwritten by highly-rated companies and our programs are designed to provide your customers with the financial security and protection they and their families deserve.

While providing peace and comfort to your customers, you can enhance your revenue, strengthen existing relationships and enhance brand loyalty. To maximize the results of your program, Coverdell offers comprehensive marketing, administration and customer service support to maximize the results of your program.

Dental, vision, prescriptions, and senior care are all examples of services provided by our membership programs.

As one of the largest providers of discount health care programs in the country, Coverdell recognizes the importance of creating strong relationships with quality, contracted medical providers. We pride ourselves on having built three primary networks – Dental, Chiropractic and Complementary and Alternative Medicine – that offer members access to thousands of credentialed practitioners across North America.

Coverdell's network providers are credentialed using the latest, state-of-the-art configurable software which provides automated verification workflows to maximize credentialing capacities and efficiencies. Our Provider Relations team manages the relationships with each provider and is responsible for ensuring the accuracy of provider information. We are committed to maintaining a network that continues to beat the expectations of our members and our clients.

UNI-CARE Dental Network:

The UNI-CARE Dental network consists of licensed individual providers and group practices across North America. Members receive savings of up to 50% on routine and preventative dental services as well as more extensive dental treatments.

UNI-CARE Chiropractic Network:

The UNI-CARE Chiropractic network is one of the largest chiropractic networks in the United States with over 9,250 licensed chiropractors. Members receive a free consultation, reduced fees for initial exams and x-rays, and 20% - 40% off the provider's usual and customary fees for diagnostic and follow-up treatments.

UNI-CARE Complementary and Alternative Medicine (CAM) Network:

The UNI-CARE CAM network provides access to a wide range of CAM practitioners for individuals interested in utilizing alternatives to traditional medical care to maintain health and wellness. Members save up to 20% on CAM services, including acupuncture, chiropractic care, massage therapy, nutritional counseling, meditation/relaxation and naturopathy.
