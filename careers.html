Careers

Embark on a new and exciting career

If you are interested in working for a company that inspires and encourages innovation, appreciates individuality and provides the opportunity to make a difference, you’ve come to the right place.

Coverdell strives to provide our employees with a first-rate work environment that fosters creativity, demands excellence and encourages diversity. Our goals are to provide our clients with customized marketing programs that create new revenue streams and increase member loyalty and to serve each client with the utmost professionalism and integrity. We are constantly seeking creative thinkers, problem solvers and team players to help us achieve our objective. In return, you can expect Coverdell to support your efforts, provide continuous learning opportunities, and motivate you to reach and surpass your potential.

In addition to a dynamic culture, Coverdell offers competitive salaries, an excellent benefits package, a friendly environment and the opportunity to work in several top cities.

Diversity Breeds Innovation

At Coverdell, diversity is the rule, not the exception. Our objective is to recruit and retain a talent pool that enables us to leverage the strengths of all employees – doers, thinkers, creators, leaders – to develop new and better ways to serve our clients. We are committed to maintaining a workplace where a variety of opinions and perspectives are accepted and expected and where employees understand they can be themselves.
