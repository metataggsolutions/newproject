Affinity Marketing

Strategy is the catalyst of success

We began our business with affinity marketing — it’s in our DNA. Since our inception, we’ve created strong relationships with and marketed through national financial institutions.

Strategy is essential to identifying and fulfilling your unique needs. Our solutions are always completely customized for your customers, compliance requirements and business goals.

As a full-service marketer and licensed plan administrator, we’ll manage all marketing efforts and back-end administration for your program. We provide our clients with a complete marketing package, from initial product options and campaign strategy to customized analysis and reporting.

Life & Health

Individual Accident Plan

Among other benefits, customers of the Individual Accident Plan receive cash benefits for:

  • Accidental death and dismemberment
  • Permanent total disability
  • Childcare expenses
  • Education expenses
  • Excess medical expenses
  • Home health care
  • In-hospital coverage

Individual Hospital Accident Plan

The Individual Hospital Accident Plan includes cash benefits for:

  • Hospital confinement due to a covered accident
  • Intensive care unit (ICU)
  • Emergency department treatment
  • Recuperation
  • Accidental death and dismemberment

Freedom Term Life

Freedom Term Life offers up to $25,000 of affordable simplified issue life insurance coverage with benefits that increase after the initial term.

Lifestyle

Personal Information Protection Program

Provides members and families with a resource to call if they suspect their identity has been compromised. Should identity theft occur, members have access to attorneys who specialize in identity restoration.

Getaway Plus

A unique travel and leisure program that allows members to travel the world and enjoy extraordinary deals on top resorts in vacation destinations all over the world.
